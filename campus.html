🏫 Campus & Safety

Everything you need to know about staying safe during your stay.

Students must not leave the Boarding Houses or the campus without permission and supervision from a member of staff.

Visits to the shops in Versoix can be arranged with the House Parent(s) on duty.

Always use the litter bins provided and help keep the Boarding Houses and the campus clean.

Keep your room tidy and presentable every morning so that the cleaning staff can carry out their work.

Smoking, vaping, drinking alcohol and the use or possession of illegal drugs are strictly prohibited.

Only prescribed medication is permitted and must be declared to the Summer Camp staff upon arrival.

The Boarding House alarm is activated every night.

Once the alarm is set, doors and windows must not be opened unless instructed by a member of staff.

The panic button must only be used in the event of a genuine emergency, such as an intruder entering the Boarding House.

When you hear the fire alarm, remain calm and leave the Boarding House immediately.

Follow the instructions of staff and proceed directly to the designated assembly point.

The lockdown code is:

LE BUS DE LAUSANNE EST EN RETARD

As soon as this message is heard, follow staff instructions immediately. If indoors, remain in a safe location. If outdoors, move away from danger and hide until the situation has been resolved.
