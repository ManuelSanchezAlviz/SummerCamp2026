🍽️ Meals

Healthy eating is an important part of the Summer Camp experience.

Students are expected to attend all scheduled meals during their stay at Collège du Léman.

House Parents monitor students' wellbeing, including eating habits and activity levels.

Meal Time
🍳 Breakfast 08:00 – 08:45
🥪 Lunch 12:30 – 13:00
🍽️ Dinner 18:30 – 19:00

Ordering takeaway food is not permitted.

Students may use the following appliances:

  • ☕ Kettle
  • 🍞 Toaster
  • ❄️ Refrigerator
  • 🍲 Microwave

The use of stoves is not permitted.

Students are expected to behave respectfully during meals, follow staff instructions and help maintain a pleasant dining environment for everyone.
