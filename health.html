🩺 Health Centre

Your health and wellbeing are our priority.

A qualified nurse is available on campus from Monday to Friday, 08:30 to 17:30.

The Health Centre is here to support students with illnesses, injuries and general medical concerns.

If you feel unwell or require medical assistance, please inform your House Parent or an Activity Leader immediately.

Do not go directly to the Health Centre unless instructed to do so by a member of staff.

Only medication prescribed by a doctor or declared to the Summer Camp staff may be kept or administered.

Never take medication belonging to another student.

In the event of a medical emergency, remain calm and immediately inform the nearest member of staff.

Our team will contact the Health Centre and, if necessary, the emergency services.

  • 💧 Stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • 🍎 Eat regular, balanced meals.
  • 😴 Get enough sleep every night.
  • 🧴 Wear sunscreen during outdoor activities.
  • 🧢 Wear a cap or hat in hot weather.
  • 😊 Speak to a member of staff if you are worried about anything.
