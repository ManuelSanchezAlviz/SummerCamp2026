🤝 Code of Conduct

Together we create a safe, respectful and welcoming international community.

Collège du Léman Summer Camp brings together students from all over the world.

Every culture, language and tradition enriches our community. We encourage every student to be curious, open-minded and respectful towards others.

By learning from one another, we create an unforgettable international experience.

  • 🤝 Treat everyone with kindness, respect and courtesy.
  • 🌍 Appreciate and respect different cultures, languages, beliefs and traditions.
  • ❤️ Be open-minded and inclusive, making everyone feel welcome.
  • 🇬🇧🇫🇷 Respect both English and French as the official languages of the Summer Camp and make every effort to communicate respectfully.
  • 👨‍🏫 Follow the instructions of House Parents, Activity Leaders and all members of staff.
  • 🏠 Respect the rights, privacy and property of others.
  • 😴 Respect the sleep and wellbeing of others by keeping noise to a minimum during quiet hours.
  • 🛡️ Behave responsibly and make safe choices at all times.
  • ⏰ Be punctual and attend all scheduled activities and meals.
  • 🧹 Help maintain a clean and welcoming environment.
  • 🏕️ Represent yourself and Collège du Léman positively both on and off campus.

Bullying, intimidation, discrimination, harassment or any behaviour that makes another person feel unsafe or unwelcome will not be tolerated.

We are committed to providing a safe, inclusive and supportive environment for every student.

Any concerns or signs of bullying will be taken seriously and addressed promptly by our staff.

Every student plays an important role in creating a positive Summer Camp experience.

By respecting one another, celebrating our differences and supporting each other, we create a community where everyone feels safe, valued and included.

Failure to follow the Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action.

Depending on the seriousness of the incident, this may include the loss of privileges, parental notification or, in exceptional circumstances, dismissal from the Summer Camp.

🏕️ Collège du Léman Summer Camp 2026

Together we inspire respect, responsibility and international friendship.
