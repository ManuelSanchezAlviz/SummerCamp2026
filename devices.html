📱 Electronic Devices

Technology helps us stay connected, but it should never distract from the Summer Camp experience.

Mobile phones and other electronic devices must not be used during activities unless permission has been given by a member of staff.

If a device is used without permission, it may be confiscated by the Activity Leader for the remainder of the activity.

Students aged 14 years and under must hand in their electronic devices at bedtime.

House Parents may also require any student to hand in their devices if they are being misused.

Students may not make or receive phone calls during meals.

Unauthorised use of mobile phones during meals may result in disciplinary action.

The following items are not permitted in student rooms:

  • Heaters
  • Cooking appliances
  • Refrigerators
  • Televisions
  • Sun lamps

Electronic devices may be taken on off-campus activities as a safety resource.

Should they be used during the activity without permission, they will be confiscated for the remainder of the activity.
