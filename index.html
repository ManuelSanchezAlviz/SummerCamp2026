Everything you need to know during your stay at Collège du Léman.

We are here to have fun. However, we also need to follow and respect some rules to ensure everyone enjoys a safe, respectful and memorable Summer Camp experience.

Students must not leave the Boarding Houses or the campus without permission and supervision from a member of staff.

Visits to the shops in Versoix can be arranged with the House Parent(s) on duty.

Always use the litter bins provided and help keep the Boarding Houses and campus clean.

Keep your room tidy and presentable every morning so that the cleaning staff can carry out their work.

Smoking, vaping, drinking alcohol and the use or possession of illegal drugs are strictly prohibited.

Only prescribed medication is permitted and must be declared to the Summer Camp staff on arrival.

The Boarding House alarm is activated every night.

Once the alarm is set, do not open any doors or windows unless instructed by a member of staff.

The panic button must only be used in a genuine emergency, such as if an intruder is seen inside the Boarding House.

Misuse of the panic button is strictly prohibited.

If you hear the fire alarm, remain calm and leave the Boarding House immediately.

Follow staff instructions and proceed directly to the designated assembly point.

Code message:

LE BUS DE LAUSANNE EST EN RETARD

Stay calm. Follow staff instructions immediately. If you are inside a building, remain in a safe place. If you are outside, move away from danger and hide safely until instructed otherwise.

Mobile devices must not be used during activities unless permission has been given by a member of staff.

If a mobile device is used without permission, the Activity Leaders will confiscate it for the remainder of the activity.

Students aged 14 and under must hand in their electronic devices at bedtime.

House Parents may also require any student to hand in their devices if they are being misused.

Mobile phones must not be used to make or receive calls during meals.

Unauthorised use may result in disciplinary action.

The following items are not permitted in student rooms:

  • Heaters
  • Cooking appliances
  • Refrigerators
  • Televisions
  • Sun lamps

Electronic devices may be taken on off-campus activities for safety and emergency communication.

They must not be used during activities unless permission has been given by a member of staff.

Students are expected to attend all scheduled meals during their stay.

House Parents monitor students' wellbeing, including eating habits and activity levels.

  • 🍳 Breakfast: 08:00 – 08:45
  • 🥪 Lunch: 12:30 – 13:00
  • 🍝 Dinner: 18:30 – 19:00

Ordering takeaway food is not permitted.

Students may use:

  • Kettle
  • Toaster
  • Microwave
  • Refrigerator

The use of stoves is not permitted.

Opening Hours

🥤 Daily from 15:15 to 16:15.

Snacks and drinks are available during this time.

Pocket money can be withdrawn every Monday, Wednesday and Friday during the scheduled breaks.

Students must bring their student ID card to collect their pocket money.

A nurse is available on campus Monday to Friday, from 08:30 to 17:30.

If you feel unwell, please inform your House Parent or an Activity Leader before visiting the Health Centre.

Each Boarding House is equipped with washing machines and dryers for student use.

Collège du Léman Summer Camp brings together students from all over the world.

We celebrate our diversity and believe that every culture, language and background enriches our community.

We ask every student to embrace this international spirit by showing curiosity, kindness and respect towards others.

  • 🤝 Treat everyone with kindness, respect and courtesy.
  • 🌍 Appreciate and respect different cultures, languages, beliefs and traditions.
  • ❤️ Be open-minded and inclusive, making everyone feel welcome.
  • 🇬🇧🇫🇷 Embrace our bilingual environment by respecting both English and French.
  • 👨‍🏫 Follow the instructions of House Parents, Activity Leaders and all members of staff.
  • 🏠 Respect the rights, privacy and property of others.
  • 😴 Respect the sleep and wellbeing of others by keeping noise to a minimum during quiet hours.
  • 🛡️ Behave responsibly and make safe choices at all times.
  • ⏰ Be punctual and attend all scheduled activities and meals.
  • 🧹 Help maintain a clean and welcoming environment.
  • 🏕️ Represent yourself and Collège du Léman positively both on and off campus.
  • 🚫 Bullying, intimidation, discrimination and harassment will not be tolerated.

Every student plays an important role in creating a friendly, inclusive and supportive community.

By respecting one another and celebrating our differences, we ensure that Summer Camp is a positive experience for everyone.

Failure to follow the Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action, including the loss of privileges or, in serious cases, dismissal from the Summer Camp.
