🧺 Laundry

Keeping your clothes clean helps you stay comfortable throughout your Summer Camp experience.

Each Boarding House is equipped with washing machines and dryers for student use.

If you are unsure how to use the machines, please ask a House Parent for assistance.

  • 👕 Check clothing labels before washing.
  • 🎨 Separate white and coloured clothing.
  • 🌡️ Use the correct washing programme.
  • 🧺 Remove your laundry promptly once finished.
  • 🧦 Make sure you collect all your belongings.

The laundry room is shared by everyone.

  • 🧹 Leave the area clean after use.
  • ⏰ Do not leave clothes in the machines for long periods.
  • 😊 Respect other students who are waiting to use the facilities.

If you need any assistance with the washing machines, dryers or laundry products, please speak to your House Parent.
