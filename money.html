💳 Pocket Money

Everything you need to know about collecting your pocket money.

Pocket money can be withdrawn on the following days:

  • 📅 Monday
  • 📅 Wednesday
  • 📅 Friday

Withdrawals take place during the scheduled breaks.

Students must present their Student ID Card before pocket money can be issued.

Without your Student ID, we cannot release your pocket money.

We encourage students to spend their money responsibly during their stay.

  • 💡 Plan your spending.
  • 🛍️ Avoid carrying large amounts of cash.
  • 🔒 Keep your money in a safe place.
  • ❓If you lose your money, inform a House Parent immediately.

If you have any questions regarding your pocket money, please speak to your House Parent.
